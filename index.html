Overview

Medical practice is the act of for the purpose of protecting the health, safety, and welfare of healthcare consumers through proper licensing and regulation of physicians and other health care practitioners. Medical practice has been around since the 6000 thousand but Modern medicine started to emerge after the Industrial Revolution in the 18th century
